# Privacy Policy for Tend

_Last updated: [DATE 26 June 2026]_

Tend ("the app", "we", "us") is a micro-meditation app published by **pudico**. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. Tend is private by design: **no accounts, no ads, and no third-party tracking.**

## Information stored on your device
Tend stores the following **only on your device** — never on our servers:

- Your reminder time(s) and on/off settings
- Your streak and last-completed date
- Your theme preference
- Whether you have completed onboarding

This data never leaves your device and is permanently removed when you uninstall the app.

## Subscriptions
Tend offers an auto-renewing subscription with a free trial. Purchases are processed by **Apple** (App Store) or **Google** (Google Play) — we never see or store your payment details.

To validate subscriptions and support "Restore Purchases", we use **RevenueCat, Inc.** RevenueCat receives a randomly generated, anonymous app-user identifier and limited purchase/device information (e.g. platform, country, subscription status). RevenueCat does **not** receive your name or email. See RevenueCat's policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Notifications
Daily reminders are scheduled **locally on your device**. We do not operate push servers and do not collect device push tokens.

## What we do NOT do
- We do not collect your name, email, or contacts.
- We do not show ads or use advertising identifiers.
- We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
- We do not sell or share your personal data.

## Children
Tend is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

## Your rights (GDPR, KVKK, and similar)
Because personal data is stored only on your device and we maintain no user accounts, you can exercise your rights — including access and erasure — by deleting the app. For subscription data held by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat, contact us at **doga.maltepe@gmail.com** and we will help where we can.

## Data processors
- **Apple Inc. / Google LLC** — app distribution and payment processing
- **RevenueCat, Inc.** — subscription validation

## Changes
We may update this policy. Material changes are reflected by the "last updated" date above.
